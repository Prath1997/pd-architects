NOC Consultancy

Statutory clearances, handled with confidence

No Objection Certificates are often the bottleneck between design and construction. PDA Architects prepares accurate applications and coordinates with Fire, Irrigation, utility providers and municipal departments—so your project advances without administrative surprises.

Utility NOCs

MSEDCL, water supply, sewage and telecom clearances coordinated in parallel.

Fire & Safety

Fire NOC documentation, occupancy requirements and inspection support.

Municipal & State

Department-specific formats, fees and follow-up until NOC is granted.

NOC Categories We Handle

Fire NOC

High-rise, assembly and commercial occupancies with Maharashtra Fire rules compliance.

Irrigation NOC

Canal, river and irrigation zone clearances for sites near blue-line areas.

CRZ Clearance

Coastal Regulation Zone applications where projects fall in designated zones.

Electrical NOC

Load calculations, substation requirements and DISCOM application support.

Water & Sewage

NMC water connection and drainage NOCs for new and revised buildings.

Tree & Environment

Tree cutting permissions and environmental compliances where triggered.
