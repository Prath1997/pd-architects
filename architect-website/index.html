About PD

PD Architects is a multidisciplinary architecture and design firm based in Nashik, established in 2012. We create inspiring and practical spaces with innovative, sustainable, and client-focused design—from homes and commercial buildings to community spaces that reflect each client’s vision.

Our Founder

Ar. Pradeep Deshmukh

Founder & Principal Architect

An architect with a strong foundation in design, construction, and spatial planning, complemented by a Master’s degree in Project Management. He manages complex architectural and construction projects on time, within budget, and aligned with client vision—bridging innovative design and efficient execution.
